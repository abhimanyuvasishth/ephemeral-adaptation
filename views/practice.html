This experiment is an evaluation of 2 menu techniques.

It consists of 2 blocks for EACH technique. Each block consists of 45 individual tasks.

In between techniques and after the experiment, we will ask you to fill out a brief form.

For each individual task, a prompt containing a menu and a word will be presented.

You will be asked to click the correct word in the correct menu, as given by the prompt.

Click the button to see an example of the task.

In this case, you should click Menu 2, then click Pistachio. If you click the wrong menu or item, you will not be able to proceed. You can only proceed to the next page when you click the right menu and item.

You should click the menu item as fast as you can.
